Crystals Collector

Choose a Crystal!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clinking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the Target. You will lose the game if your total score goes above the Target

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it and it will change each round.
